Super Sparty Maker lets you build your own Super Sparty Bros Levels.

  • Click on the tile you want in your game from the bottom grid
  • Move It in the game canvas using WASD or the ARROW keys
  • Move the camera using the 4 blue buttons on the top
  • Move through every tile using the 3 orange byttons above the tile grid
  • Rotate the tile by clicking on it with the RIGTH MOUSE BUTTON
  • Select tile that are already on the canvas clicking on it with the LEFT MOUSE BUTTON
  • When you are ready, press PLAY and enjoy your level
